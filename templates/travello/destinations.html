Resources for Managing Work and Life During COVID-19

The COVID-19 virus is changing the world in which we live and work. The pandemic has prompted a wide range of businesses to (at least temporarily) ask their employees to work from home. It restricts us all from enjoying our normal activities and spending time with friends, family, and others.
Our commitment to society:

To help you make the best of your situation, we’d like to share a growing list of tips and stories from DSoft and the wider DSoft community. We hope you find them inspiring, useful, or simply a nice distraction. Kind request to subscribe to our newsletter and community

On giving back:

Participate in Active for Good’s monthly activity challenges and make an impact. It's free — and every minute of your activity goes towards unlocking meals for malnourished children.

On working from home:

Providing best access speed in the world all the time to help you work effortlessly from home as if you are in your office.When I mention our remote to onsite ratio to friends, I get the same look of incredulity normally followed by a “how does that work?!”. The truth is it works great. We’re productive, communicative, and happy, but that’s not to say it’s easy or without its sacrifices.

Environment

Environment is the other part of the equation. That’s all changed. Most people who have figured out how to successfully work from home have tech arrangements that support their effectiveness.

Without robust technology support, don’t expect the same performance.

It takes time to learn to how to work effectively from home. What training is the company providing? Are people addressing how to have effective remote meetings?. Contact us to know more.....

Our thoughts are with all those who have been affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.
